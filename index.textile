h1(color1). welcome to dicto

Dicto is software designed for vocabulary learning. It allows convinient and effective learning, and doesn't annoy with unnesessary functions or effects.

With dicto you'll easily learn vocabulary, dates or any key-pair data for your exams!

Dicto is released under "GNU GPL":http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.txt license, so you can download, modify or spread the word without limits! If you're running GNU/Linux, there's dicto version for this platform too.
